About Us

Three brothers. One purpose.

Selah Watch Co. is Kevin, Ricky, and Kris — three brothers with a shared passion for watches. We created this company to share that love: buying and reselling from vintage to modern, entry level to luxury, with Japanese and Swiss movements. Selah means to pause and reflect — and that’s the spirit behind every piece we place in someone’s hands.
